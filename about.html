About TSF

Trenchkid Support Foundation exists to empower underprivileged children with education, digital skills, and mentorship—enabling them to build independent and impactful futures.

DONATE See our impact

Core mission

TSF is built around empowerment: giving children opportunity, skills, and mentorship so they can build strong futures.

1) Break the cycle of poverty

Many children are talented but lack school fees, mentorship, exposure, and basic needs. TSF exists to provide opportunity—not just charity, but empowerment.

2) Provide education & skill development

We support schooling, teach digital skills, encourage entrepreneurship, and build confidence. Not just giving food—but giving future.

3) Build responsible future leaders

The aim isn’t just helping kids survive. It’s helping them become technicians, developers, business owners, leaders, and change-makers.

4) Create a community that cares

We mobilize volunteers, partner with schools and tech communities, and encourage youth mentorship.
